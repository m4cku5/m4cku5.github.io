Hello, world! My name is Michael, and I'm what one might call a "generalist", who strives to achieve
"Renaissance man" status in his lifetime. I can probably fix your car, and I can certainly fix your
computer. I might build a table one week, a website the next, or I might throw a dinner party and grill
the best goddamn steak you've ever had. I'm a firm believer in balance, whether it be work/life,
fiction/non-fiction, or socializing/solitude. I enjoy the occasional film, but I prefer
[books]({% link books.md %}). I like video games as much as the next g33k, but I played enough of them
in to my 20's to last a lifetime, so I set limits. I've traveled a bit (even lived in Paris for 5
years), but I'd like to see more, and on a regular basis. I love the outdoors and used to hike 3-5 miles
nearly every morning, while in community college, and daydream of getting back to that. I'm happily
married to a superhero, am father to a ninja, and coexist with a serial killer of blue jays. I can
count to ten in six languages, and swear at you in three. I try to stay fit, torturing myself with
calisthenics and dumbbells, and am toying with the idea of training for a triathlon. I'm terrible at
paragraphs and I can't quite seem to go underwater without holding my nose. Bourbon, neat. I prefer to
leave parties without saying goodbye, and to leave my writings open-ended.
